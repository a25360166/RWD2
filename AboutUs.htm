Hestia Bakery landed in Taipei Xianor Building in early 1998 to become one of the best quality bakery shop. Since Mr. Peter Lee found a smart technology for giving a unforgettable tasty on the bread, we believe what we want to provide to our customers is not only the physical fulless but also the mental satisfication.

Hestia Bakery opened its first bakery in Taipei, Xianor Building - a stunning location which is located on the heart of Taipei, in 1998. Encouraged by France baker's enthusiatic response, our main founder of Hestia Bakery opens the second branch in Taipei main district after three years open of the first Hestia.

We believe everybody deserves to enjoy good bread every single day. That's why we bake slow fermentation sourdough breads for our customers in and around Taipei - insisting on a few simple ingredient and use organic flour We can't imagine doing anything more gratifying than baking our, and your, bread.
